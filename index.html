What Looks Broken on Phone

Captured at 390×844 (iPhone-class). These are the first things a mobile visitor hits.

Critical

Map region is a blank 555px slab. Tailwind !h-[555px] wins over the mobile CSS that tries to set #map to 280px — and when Maps fails or lags, users get a huge empty hole.

Critical

Hero line uses whitespace-nowrap. “A DAVE PURCHASE PROJECT INITIATIVE” cannot wrap, so it clips/overflows on narrow screens.

High

Directory hides City + State under 549px. .cityCol / .stateCol are display:none — names only, no location context on the list people came for.

High

587-row table dumped to the phone. No pagination, cards, or progressive disclosure — hard to scan, heavy to load.

Med

Title paints as stacked cyan “ticket” boxes. Line-box backgrounds on the H1-styled paragraph make the intro look fragmented, not intentional.
